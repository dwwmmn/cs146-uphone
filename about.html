About Us

DragonFruit, Inc. was created by five undergraduates at Stevens Institute of Technology in Hoboken, New Jersey. Three of the five student are currently Computer Science majors, while two of the five are majoring in CyberSecurity. The goal of the team is to create a phone that reflects what consumers really want in a phone, as opposed to what they think consumers want.
