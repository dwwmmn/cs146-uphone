What is the uPhone?

The uPhone is a revolutionary open-source hardware and software combination which will be completely developed based on what you need in a phone. To start off the project, we'll be doing crowd funding and sourcing on a massive scale to ensure that the people interested in the project will get a phone that is tailored to their greatest needs, and can be modified to a perfect fit if so desired.

About Us

DragonFruit, Inc. is a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing you an excellent experience. We are a mix of software and hardware engineers passionate about mobile devices. We have an ethnically and professionally diverse team. All of us at DragonFruit feel ready and excited to start working on the uPhone project. You can read more about our team here.
